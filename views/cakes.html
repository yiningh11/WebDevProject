We do Custom Cakes!

Our custom made cakes are order only. We suggest that you order as early as possible for your event as we only schedule a limited number of cakes each week. This is because of the time it takes to create a cake for our customers. Cakes are booked according to our schedule. PLEASE NOTE: our cakes and icing are made fresh with quality ingredients and beautifully decorated by hand. We put 3 layers of filling in each tier of our round and square cakes, so our cakes are bigger than the grocery stores and therefore cost more. One layer sheet cakes are not filled, two layer sheets are filled between the layers. Basic cake flavors: vanilla, chocolate, almond, strawberry, banana, red velvet. Other flavors are available upon request. An additional charge may apply.
